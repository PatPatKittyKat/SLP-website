Values


"The greatest reward of being a Speech Language Pathologist is to see the progress made by the people who receive my services. My approach is to develop individual plans and involve the family members in the process. At the end, I get to share the pride and success stories of my clients' progress in communication skills. I love what I do!"


GOAL: My goal is to maximize each individual's potential in communication skills, one word at a time.

VISION: One day, the "LJHC Speech Pathology Services Foundation" will be a "Pay-It-Forward" clinic where every individual in need of intervention within the Speech Pathology scope of care will be able to receive quality services from qualified, lincensed Speech Pathologists.
