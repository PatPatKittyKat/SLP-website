About   LJHC

After receiving her Master of Science degree in Speech Pathology from Texas Tech University in 1980, LeeJane continues to receive advanced training to extend her skillset. By attending professional conferences at the state and national levels, she keeps up-to-date with the most current researched studies to complement her 30+ years of practical experience.

LeeJane's extensive clinical experience includes working at rehabilitation hospitals in the Houston Medical Center, Humble, and NW Houston areas, home health settings, public school settings, head start programs, and private clinics.

Being a three-time recipient of the ASHA's prestigious Award for Continuing Education (ACE) is proof of her compassion in the field of Speech Pathology. In addition, numerous positive testimonials from past clients and their family members are the results of her dedication to her patients.

MEMBERSHIPS  &  PROFESSIONAL AFFILIATIONS:

  • American Speech and Hearing Association (ASHA)
  • Texas Speech and Hearing Association (TSHA)
  • Houston Association for Communication Disorders
  • ASHA Special Interest Group 03: Voice and Voice Disorders
  • ASHA Special Interest Group 14: Culture and Linguistic Diversity
  • ASHA Special Interest Group 18: Telepractice
